Endodontics

With today's modern dental techniques, having root canals done by a dentist is no longer a scary experience.

Locate a dentist who is experienced in performing root canal therapy.

Extensive decay, an accident, tooth fracture, or gum disease can kill teeth and cause a root canal symptom such as root canal pain or a dental abscess. However, regardless of the root canal cause, your teeth can be saved using modern therapy to do root canal treatment.
